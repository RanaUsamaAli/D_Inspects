DATA SCIENCES

This professional training course is designed to equip participants with the necessary skills to succeed in the data science field. The course is open to all, regardless of their background in computer science or programming. Upon completion of the course, participants will have gained valuable knowledge, tools, and a portfolio that will enable them to excel as entry-level data scientists. With the high demand for data scientists, this course is ideal for anyone interested in pursuing a career in this field.
